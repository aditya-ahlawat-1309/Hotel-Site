Our Specials

Shudh Food Court is one of the recognized venture of Swati Group which has got experience of more than three decades in operating Government Licensed lotteries across India entered into hospitality industry with the inclusion of two hotels “Swati” and “Swati Deluxe” and two restaurants namely “Suruchi” and “Shudh” one in each hotel. Both units are situated in city centre of New Delhi at Karol Bagh.

Swati Hotel is the first pearl in the necklace of Hospitality section of Swati Group. It has 28 beautifully decorated rooms.

Swati Deluxe has 44 rooms with more facilities and added features. Suruchi Restaurant is a destination for the food lovers who look for authentic Gujrati, Rajasthani cuisine and Mumbai snacks. The Suruchi Restaurant, serve the finest traditional Indian cuisine served in traditional style.

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to The Frying Pan, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Peter Pan, that featured for the first time the world's best cuisines in a pan.
